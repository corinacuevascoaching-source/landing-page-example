Assessments & Leadership Insights

Assessments can be integrated into coaching engagements to deepen self-awareness and support intentional development. Tools are selected thoughtfully based on individual or organizational goals.

Energy Leadership Index™

An attitudinal assessment that surfaces how you show up under everyday stress and helps you intentionally shift into more effective leadership states.

Clifton Strengths-Based Assessments

Tools that identify and amplify natural strengths so leaders can contribute with greater ease, impact, and authenticity.

HBDI

Herrmann Brain Dominance Instrument to illuminate thinking preferences, communication styles, and opportunities to collaborate across differences.

Hogan

The Hogan Assessment is a suite of scientifically validated personality tests — covering everyday behavior (HPI), stress-induced derailers (HDS), and core values (MVPI) — used by organizations to predict workplace performance, guide hiring decisions, and develop leadership talent.

360° Feedback

Structured feedback from key stakeholders that highlights strengths, blind spots, and practical opportunities for targeted growth.

Important: Assessments are never used in isolation and are always debriefed and integrated through coaching conversations.
